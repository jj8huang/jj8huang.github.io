Weather To Bring It

Created at HackWestern IV. This web application gives the user the weather forecast for the next week in any city in the world. It gives details about the temperature, the precipitation, and the wind speed. In addition, it will suggest to the user what to bring that day. Is it raining? Then perhaps bring an umbrella. Oh but maybe it’s too windy that day? It would be a better idea to just bring your rain coat so you don’t have to buy a new umbrella!

Tools: ReactJS
